Bacon Pancakes

cartoon network

Ingredients

  • 285g all purpose flour
  • 35g sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking poweder
  • Kosher salt
  • 2 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 Tbsp melted butter
  • Bacon
  • Maple syrup

Method

  1. In a bowl, combine 285 grams of all purpose flour, 35 grams of sugar, 1 ½ tsp each of baking powder and baking soda and some kosher salt. Whisk to combine.
  2. In the middle of the mixture create a litte well and add 2 ½ cups of buttermilk along with 2 eggs and 3 Tbsp of melted butter. Whisk to combine. Be sure not to over whisk. Lumps are okay
  3. In a cast iron skillet, melt 1 Tbsp of butter over a medium high heat before adding your pancake batter.
  4. Before flipping, add some strips of bacon and then flip.
  5. Make 2-3 pancakes before topping with butter and maple syrup.
